Who is Ramtin?

A musician who is mostly passionate about film & video game music, currently living in the Netherlands. Studied Physics at university, and became a computer programmer and worked in a few failed startups! After graduation, started composing Cinematic style Hybrid-Orchestral musics. In recent years he moved to live in a very special place, the Hormuz island. It was there that all his previous, completely material views changed, and he became more involved in Art, Philosophy and Spirituality.
